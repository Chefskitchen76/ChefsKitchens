Who is the most famous chef in the world?





Gordon Ramsay

Internationally renowned, multi-Michelin starred chef Gordon Ramsay has opened a string of successful restaurants across the globe, from the UK and France to Singapore and the United States.

If you want more information about Gordon Ramsay VISIT THE SITE Gordon Ramsay









Marco Pierre White

Marco Pierre White is a British chef, restaurateur, and television personality.He was the first British chef to be awarded three Michelin stars. He is known for his fiery temper and his no-nonsense approach to cooking









Jamie Oliver

Jamie Oliver is a British chef, TV personality, and campaigner for healthy eating.He is known for his casual cooking style and his passion for food education. He has made a significant impact on the way people think about food and has inspired millions to cook at home

Type Description
Neapolitan pizza A traditional pizza from Naples, Italy. It is made with a thin crust, tomato sauce, and mozzarella cheese.
New York-style pizza A large, thin-crust pizza with a lot of cheese. It is often served with tomato sauce and toppings such as pepperoni, sausage, mushrooms, and onions.
